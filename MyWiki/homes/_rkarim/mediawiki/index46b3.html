Facts

Episode 4 (Humans)

  • A buzzy mosquito can never give you malaria. It is the female quieter mosquito that spreads malaria. However, the male buzzing mosquito can give you yellow fever or dengue.
  • Over half of the people who have ever lived on earth were killed by mosquitoes!

Episode 8 (Hypothetical)

  • How would you weigh your head - dip it in a bucket of water and measure displacement of water. Brain's density is very close to that of water
  • If you put a white-furred cat in a fridge for a long time (i.e. weeks), its fur would turn black - ouch!
  • How would you explain to an alien in another planet using radio communications of what is left or right? - You couldn't as it is impossible to semantically define left or right without a common point of reference. To aliens over a radio communication, establishing a common point of reference would be impossible.

QI G series (Geography)

  • No one's ever managed to find out why parrots only mimic humans but not other birds in the wild.
  • Everyone in britain is at most 70 miles from the coast.
  • War is God's way of teaching americans geography.
