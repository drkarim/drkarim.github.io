Introduction

Computes the atria thickness and reports from 0 - 10 SD, what percentage of pixels are above SD

Usage 

percentatria -i1 <lge_gipl> -i2 <la_seg> -t <wall thickness in pixels> -o <textfile output> -aw <atrial wall modified (optional)>

Versions

  • Version 1.0 - The first version computes mean and std of at wall (computed from EDT) and reports percentage of pixels above 0-10 SD of this mean.
  • Version 2.0 - This version computes maximum intensity robust to noisy pixels, takes 40% of the maximum and mean of intensities less than that. Reports percentage of pixels 0-10 of this mean
