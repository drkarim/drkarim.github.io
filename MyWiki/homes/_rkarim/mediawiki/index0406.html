Plan

  • Two sections in the T experience page: past experience from T and experience collected from claims and T-S links. T-S links need to be modified to include more info.
  • C gets asked about a T and must be able to gauge experience from this page. Say how many hours on a subject, level, school-entrance or board? Also see past experience or present experience accumulated from other teaching activities.

Modify system for data collection

Admin

  • Collect data on T experience. For each Tut and Fam link, have as many sets of the following:
    • Subject
    • level
    • scholarship
    • exam-board
    • schools

For example D teaches the T family, one kid taught at 11+ GCSE and another westminster scholarship.

  • The ability to enter ratios for each Tut-Fam link when there is more than one of the above sets. The claims hours are split based on this ratio to each set.

Current tutors

  • Can enter their past or present experience on subjetcs, levels, scholarship, school-entrance, board, etc.

New tutor registration

  • Tut registration system, check that current registration system after acceptance allows fields to be automatically filled from what T filled.
  • Also also allow past experience to be entered at this point

Design considerations

  • The design of the tutor_experience table is in such a way that future fields can be added. For now there is only school, level and subject,level. In future, one can add an extra set entity,level, and keep these as null for existing records.
