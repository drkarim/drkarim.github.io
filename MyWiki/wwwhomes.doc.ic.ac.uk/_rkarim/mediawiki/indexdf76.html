Talk

I was invited to give a talk on Data processing and visualization for MR-guided EP procedures. I spoke on the image processing techniques I have developed over the years for LGE quantification, especially my works published in IEEE JHTEM and JCMR. For visualisation, I demonstrated potential applications of my work on unfolding geometry of 3D hearts published in CMIG

The session was a chaired by Prof. Tobias Schaeffter. Here is a PDF version of my talk
