Convolution Neural Networks

My notes on convolution neural networks

Hyper-parameters

  • The tuning of hyper-parameters such as conv. filter window-size, stride, number of convolution layers and where to place max-pooling can be very sensitive to accuracy of the conv. network.
  • A very large window size combined with a large stride can compress the image resulting in smaller feature maps, but this will result in poor image recognition
  • A very small window size combined with a small stride will result in larger feature maps, yielding higher image recognition at the expense of high computational cost
