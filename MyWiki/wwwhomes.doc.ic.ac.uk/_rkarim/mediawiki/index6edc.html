Talk

My two pennies worth on what we should do to translate computer algorithms into clinical diagnosis of heart ailments.

Here I was invited to talk on the automated evaluation of LGE segmentation algorithms and how they can compared effectively. This is the cDEMRIS challenge we helped organise at the ISBI 2012 annual meeting.
