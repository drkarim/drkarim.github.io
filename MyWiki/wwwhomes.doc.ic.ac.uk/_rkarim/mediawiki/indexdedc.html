Other interesting work

Tutte's spring-embedding

  • One popular Mesh parameterization on convex planes followed from a theorem proved by Tutte. He showed that if the boundary of the mesh is embedded as a convex shape in the plane, the position of each interior vertex is a convex combination of those of its neighbors, and edges are drawn as straight lines – the result will be a planar embedding with convex faces.
  • Tutte's theorem was more mathematical (graph theory) and he didn't apply it to problems in Comp. graphics. M.S. Floater worked on many variants of this Tutte's theorem and applied to problems in mesh parameterization. One of his frequently cited papers is here. This is an excellent read.
  • What seems like an implementation of Tutte's spring-embedding mesh parameterization here on ITK insight by Arnaud Gelad
