Standard template

Fusion step

  • To register the unfold of the mean-truncated * template to the Standard template unfold, a Landmark-based registration is currently performed using a Landmark-registration-only version of my cartofuse program. 
cartofuse_lm.exe -w .
  • Corresponding points are selected on the circular borders of the two unfolds.
  • The data is transferred to the Standard template unfold using the 'Data to target' button on Cartofuse_lm program.
